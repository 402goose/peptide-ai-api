# Product Brief for Peptide AI

## 1. Executive Summary

Peptide AI has successfully engaged users by providing clear and user-friendly information about peptides, achieving a satisfactory overall user experience. However, users consistently request more detailed and personalized guidance, particularly concerning dosing, safety, and stacking protocols, which presents a significant opportunity for enhancement.

## 2. Satisfaction Metrics

- **Overall Satisfaction Score**: 7.0
- **Return Rate**: 100% of personas indicated they would return.
- **Personas at Target (7+)**: All 8 personas achieved a satisfaction score of 7.

## 3. What's Working Well

1. **User-Friendly Interface**  
   - **Personas Mentioned**: Marcus, Elena, Alex
   - The app's interface is praised for its ease of navigation and clarity.

2. **Clear Introduction to Peptides**  
   - **Personas Mentioned**: Jake, Marcus, Mike
   - Users appreciate the straightforward and accessible information provided.

3. **Non-Judgmental and Reassuring Tone**  
   - **Personas Mentioned**: Sarah, Jennifer
   - The tone of the app is supportive and reassuring, especially concerning sensitive topics like weight loss and health anxiety.

1. **Lack of Detailed Dosing and Stacking Protocols**  
   - **Personas Mentioned**: Jake, Marcus, Alex
   - **Quotes**: "I would like more detailed dosing protocols..." - Jake; "I found the lack of detailed stacking protocols and timing recommendations frustrating." - Marcus

2. **Insufficient Evidence and Study References**  
   - **Personas Mentioned**: David, Jake
   - **Quotes**: "The lack of robust human trial data was concerning." - David

3. **Side Effect and Safety Information**  
   - **Personas Mentioned**: Jennifer, Sarah
   - **Quotes**: "I would have liked more thorough information on side effects..." - Jennifer

## 5. Feature Requests

1. **Personalized Stacking and Dosing Recommendations**  
   - **Personas Mentioned**: Jake, Marcus, Elena, Alex
   - Users desire an interactive feature for creating stacks based on individual needs and goals.

2. **Comprehensive Research and Evidence-Based Information**  
   - **Personas Mentioned**: David, Elena
   - Requests for more detailed citations and evidence-backed research to support claims.

3. **User Testimonials and Case Studies**  
   - **Personas Mentioned**: Jake, Mike
   - A need for real-world testimonials to validate peptide effectiveness.

4. **Side Effect Management and Safety Tips**  
   - **Personas Mentioned**: Sarah, Jennifer
   - Suggestions for sections dedicated to managing side effects and safety considerations.

## 6. Persona-Specific Insights

- **Jake (Healing Beginner)**: More detailed dosing protocols and side effect information could elevate satisfaction.
- **Sarah (Weight Loss Mom)**: Detailed side effect management and clear comparisons of weight loss options would help.
- **David (Skeptical Researcher)**: Providing robust human trial data and direct study citations could increase satisfaction.
- **Marcus (Bodybuilder Advanced)**: Comprehensive stacking protocols and timing recommendations are crucial for higher satisfaction.
- **Elena (Biohacker Longevity)**: Personalized stack recommendations and cutting-edge research articles would be beneficial.
- **Jennifer (Anxious Cautious)**: Enhanced side effect profiles and anxiety-related FAQs could improve her experience.
- **Alex (Cognitive Optimizer)**: Specific dosing recommendations and long-term safety information are needed.
- **Mike (Budget Practical)**: Recommendations based on budget and effectiveness could enhance his experience.

## 7. Recommended Actions

1. **Develop Personalized Stacking and Dosing Features**  
   - *Impact*: Increase satisfaction by catering to individual needs and goals.

2. **Enhance Research and Evidence Section**  
   - *Impact*: Address concerns about credibility and scientific backing, particularly for skeptical users.

3. **Integrate User Testimonials and Case Studies**  
   - *Impact*: Provide social proof and real-world validation to enhance trust.

4. **Expand Side Effect and Safety Information**  
   - *Impact*: Reassure users concerned about safety, particularly those with health anxieties.

5. **Create Interactive Features for Customization**  
   - *Impact*: Improve engagement by allowing users to tailor their peptide experiences.
